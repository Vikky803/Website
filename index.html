What's actually running

Every system, one process.

Melon replaces a stack of single-purpose bots. These are the systems live in production.

01

Antinuke & Security

Blocks mass channel/role deletion, unauthorized webhook creation, and unapproved bot joins in real time, with per-server whitelists.

02

Moderation

Ban, kick, mute, timed roles, slowmode, lockdown, bulk purge with filters, and full voice moderation.

03

Automod

Invite/URL filtering, anti-spam, mass-mention detection — per-channel and per-role whitelisting.

04

Audit Logging

Message edits/deletes with content snapshots, server changes, member events, and voice state — routed per event type.

05

AI Assistant

Groq-backed chat with Gemini vision and live web search, on a per-channel toggle, running under its own identity.

06

Ticketing

Multi-category tickets with staff routing, claim/transfer/rename/close/reopen, and full transcripts.

07

Giveaways

Custom prize, duration, and winner count, with early-end and reroll at any time.

08

Welcome & Farewell

Rich embeds with image/background support and a test command to preview before going live.

09

Profiles & Leaderboards

Canvas-rendered profile cards with bio and social links, plus global message and invite leaderboards.

10

Automation

Join-to-Create voice channels, autopost, autobump, autoreact, vanity roles, and reaction roles.
